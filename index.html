How all started...

In 2017,a small person from a small non-European country comes to avanced Germany.He comes to a small private school to learn the language and culture.But apart that he discovers a new world,almost a new planet.After 9 months,he leaves.He leaves a completly different person.With different worldviews and princeples.And with a fierce desire to move on and develop further.After that,he emigrates to Europe to eventually realize his potencial.And at some point he has a dream.To establish a school that will help young people get to know themselves better,understad their place in this world,find their potencial and understand how to realise it.But so,that it would be an unforgettable and exciting adventure.This story is true.And this is a story about me.Let me introduce myself.My name is Rafael Ardo.
